Sacred Wisdom from Hindu Scriptures
Bhagavad Gita · 10:20
"I am the Self, seated in the hearts of all creatures. I am the beginning, the middle and the end of all beings."
— God lives in every human being. Your body is His temple. Your breath is His gift.
Bhagavad Gita · 7:9
"I am the pure fragrance of the earth and the brightness in fire. I am the life in all existences."
— Every heartbeat, every breath is God expressing Himself through you.
Chandogya Upanishad · 6.8.7
"Tat tvam asi" — That Thou Art.
— You are not separate from God. The Divine that created all things also created you.
Rigveda · 1.164.46
"Ekam sad viprā bahudhā vadanti" — Truth is One; the wise call it by many names.
— All paths lead to the same God. Your name, face, flower — all are His fingerprints.
Samudrik Shastra
"The body is a map of the soul's journey. Every feature placed by the Divine reveals the purpose of this incarnation."
— Your face is not random. It was given by God as a reflection of your inner divine quality.
Vishnu Purana · 1.12
"Every being that lives, every flower that blooms, every name spoken — all emerge from the one Divine consciousness."
— Your existence is not an accident. It is God's most precise and intentional creation.
